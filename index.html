Origin story

Trained to design
buildings.
Ended up designing
products.

Architecture school teaches you something most design courses skip: how things are actually built. Every decision has a consequence. Structure matters. Systems matter. The person who eventually has to live in it matters most.


That thinking followed me into product. I don't just make screens look good — I think about how they connect, how they scale, how they feel to the person navigating them at 11pm on a slow connection.

"The best products, like the best buildings, make complexity invisible. That's the job."
